## Why it is needed

## Original effect to preserve

The HR workflow should support evidence organization and planning while preserving:

- no real personal data in public artifacts;
- no automatic hiring, rejection, compensation, or offer decision;
- no public export of real candidate or intern records;
- no direct write into the real workforce-war-room without a fresh private check and Kevin approval.

- become an automation;
- invite a real candidate;
- set pay or role level;
- touch real applicant, intern, or workforce-war-room data.
