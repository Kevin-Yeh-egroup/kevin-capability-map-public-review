## Public Wording Fit

The public wording should emphasize:

- 「目前定位」 rather than a final title.
- 「證據缺口」 rather than overclaiming.
- 「production proof」 and 「growth experiment」 rather than abstract skill lists.
- 「public-safe portfolio」 rather than exposing private records.

## Risk Notes

| Risk | Mitigation |
| --- | --- |
| Application content includes large goals and effect claims | Mark them as goals or claims until method validation is completed. |
| Measurement work may touch external accounts | Public page only describes proof categories; external account changes require Kevin approval. |
| People architecture may be misread as automatic evaluation | Page states that human review keeps high-impact decisions outside automation. |
| Public noindex may be mistaken for privacy | Page and logs state that noindex is search control, not access protection. |
