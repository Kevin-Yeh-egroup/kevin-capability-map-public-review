## Should Not Do

- Do not collect real user financial records in the public repo.
- Do not publish actual amounts, account identifiers, private messages, or private URLs.
- Do not recommend financial products.
- Do not make investment, borrowing, insurance, legal, or welfare eligibility decisions.
- Do not contact outside parties.
- Do not remove noindex controls.
